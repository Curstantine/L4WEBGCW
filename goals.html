Our Mission,
Commitment and Goals

Our Mission

We hold the conviction that water serves as a crucial catalyst in breaking the chains of poverty, safeguarding lives, and nurturing hopeful futures. Our mission is centered on democratizing access to water and sanitation, striving to ensure its safety, availability, and affordability for all.

We advocate for universal access to clean water and sanitation facilities while promoting sustainable practices. Through strategic partnerships, community engagement, and innovative solutions, we're committed to making significant strides towards achieving these goals, ensuring a healthier, more equitable world for generations to come.

Our Commitment

Through our commitment to United Nations' Sustainable Development Goal 6, we pledge to enhance access to safe drinking water and sanitation facilities, ensuring the well-being and dignity of communities.

By prioritizing sustainable water management practices and promoting hygiene education, we aim to safeguard public health, preserve ecosystems, and foster resilience against water-related challenges.

Our Goals

Our goals aligned with the UN Sustainable Development Goal 6, are clear and ambitious. We adopted targets 1, 2, and 3, focusing on universal access to clean water and sanitation, water quality and wastewater treatment, and water-use efficiency.

Through our initiatives, we aim to address these targets comprehensively, working towards a future where clean water and proper sanitation are accessible to all, while ensuring sustainability and efficient resource management.

1. Safe and Affordable Drinking Water
2. End Open Defecation and Provide Access To Sanitization and Hygiene
3. Improve Water Quality, Wastewater Treatment and Safe Reuse
